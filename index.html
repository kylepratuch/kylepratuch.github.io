About Kyle

I'm a web developer living in beautiful Portland, OR. I recently finished the PHP/JavaScript/Drupal track at Epicodus and am eager to flex and further strenghten these new dev muscles.

With a background in education, I know that there are always problems to solve and communities to serve. Technology provides us many opportunities to help and support each other, and I'm thrilled to be joining the world of Portland tech. When I'm not coding, I spend my time riding, working on, and occasionally racing my bicycles. Or doing something awesome with my amazing wife and our ridiculous dog. Or playing video games. Or drinking some great local beer. Usually, I'm doing more than one of these things at once.
